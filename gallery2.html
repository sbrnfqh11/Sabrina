Backstory of our trip to Japan!୭ ˚. ᵎᵎ

My family wanted to experience the cold weather and snow so we decided to go to Japan on winter season. We went there from the day before Christmas and return only after New Year! We stayed in Tokyo for 4 days, then ride the express train to Kyoto for 2 days and then we took the overnight bus for 5 hours to go to Osaka and stayed there for 4 more days. Though in brutally honest aspect, it was very tiring as we move from place to place by walking and mostly using the public transport, it was still such an exciting experience to be in another country with totally different weather and culture. The decision to go vacation to this country was made abruptly and in such a short time, hence the reason we did not have much plan in our itinerary. We couldn't go to some places as most of it need to have prior reservations. The average temperature there was 10oC - 5oC in the day and could get down to around -4oC at night.
